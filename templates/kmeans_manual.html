3. Iteration Process (3 Iterations)

Iteration 1
Euclidean Distance Formula:
d = √((x₂ - x₁)² + (y₂ - y₁)²)
Example Calculation:
Distance from Point 1 (25, 30293) to C1 (25, 30000):
d = √((25-25)² + (30293-30000)²)
d = √(0 + 293²)
d = 293

Euclidean distances were calculated and each point was assigned to the nearest centroid.

Centroids after Iteration 1: C1 (25, 30,293) — C2 (40.5, 51,484) — C3 (54, 79,324)

New centroids were calculated by averaging all points assigned to each cluster.
